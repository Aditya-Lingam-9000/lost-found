Why Use Campus Finder?

Every year, thousands of items like IDs, electronics, and books are lost on campus. Our platform creates a centralized, secure hub where you can report what you've found or what you're missing. By using an AI matching system, we automatically connect owners with finders seamlessly, eliminating the hassle of campus-wide email blasts.

If You Lost Something

  • Step 1: Sign In - Log in securely using your College Google Account.
  • Step 2: Submit a Report - Provide details like item name, description, and the last seen location from your dashboard.
  • Step 3: Wait for AI Match - Our system constantly checks reported found items and notifies you if a high-confidence match is detected.
  • Step 4: Verify & Meet - Start a secure chat to verify details, then arrange a safe handover.

If You Found Something

  • Step 1: Sign In - Log in securely.
  • Step 2: Upload Details - Post an image and description of the item you found entirely from your dashboard.
  • Step 3: AI Processing - The system cross-references your item with user lost reports locally in fractions of a second.
  • Step 4: Return It - Answer the verification chat and schedule a meetup to return the property.
